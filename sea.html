Black Sea

The waters of the Black Sea are clearer, deeper and cooler, and the coast with equipped sandy and pebble beaches is much more picturesque and diverse, in contrast to the predominantly steppe landscapes of Azov. Due to the cold currents, jellyfish can often be found in local waters, and the water temperature can be quite cool even on a hot day in midsummer. In Odessa, Kherson and Nikolaev regions there are many mud baths and sanatoriums, so here you can not only fully relax, but also improve your health.
